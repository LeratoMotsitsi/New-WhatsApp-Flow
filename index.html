🛡️ Insurance & Protection

▶️
1:00
"Insurance protects you from financial disasters you can't afford to handle alone. Think of it as buying peace of mind. Start with the basics: medical aid or hospital plan, and life insurance if others depend on your income. Car insurance is legally required if you drive. Don't over-insure or under-insure—find the balance. Read policies carefully and understand what's covered. Insurance isn't about making money—it's about not losing everything when bad things happen. Compare quotes annually, and remember: the cheapest option isn't always the best if it doesn't cover what you need."

⚖️ Risk Management

▶️
1:00
"Risk management is about preparing for what could go wrong before it does. Identify your biggest financial risks: losing your job, getting sick, car breaking down. Then create plans to minimize impact. Diversify your income if possible—side hustles, skills development. Keep important documents safe and accessible. Build relationships and networks for support. Don't put all your eggs in one basket—whether it's jobs, investments, or banks. The goal isn't to eliminate all risk—that's impossible—but to be prepared and resilient. Smart risk management turns potential disasters into manageable challenges."
